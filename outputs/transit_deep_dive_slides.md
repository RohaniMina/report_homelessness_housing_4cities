# Transit Ridership Recovery After COVID

- Metros: **NYC, SF Bay, Chicago**
- Source: **FTA NTD complete monthly ridership (`8bui-9xvu`)**
- Window: Through **2025-12**
- Baseline: **2015-2019** (same-month normalization)

---

# TL;DR

- NYC recovered furthest: **89.9%** of baseline (Dec 2025)
- SF Bay and Chicago lag: **66.7%** and **62.0%**
- All three hit trough in **April 2020**
- 2025 annual vs 2019 remains below full recovery in every metro

# Strategic Implications

- Move from "systemwide recovery" to **metro+mode-specific planning**
- Protect high-recovery frequent services; redesign weak commuter peaks
- Track leading indicators monthly by mode and corridor

---

# Appendix: Data and Method

- Data: Agency-reported UPT (monthly), mode-level
- Aggregation: UZA metro labels for NYC/SF Bay/Chicago
- Baseline metric: each month vs same month average (2015-2019)
- Recovery metric: latest month and latest 12-month averages vs baseline
