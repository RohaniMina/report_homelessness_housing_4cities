Executive Summary: Public Transit Recovery After COVID

NYC, SF Bay, Chicago | FTA NTD dataset 8bui-9xvu | Through 2025-12

Bottom Line

  • NYC recovered substantially more than SF Bay and Chicago.
  • All three collapsed in April 2020, but post-shock recovery paths diverged sharply.
  • By end-2025, no metro fully returned to 2019 annual totals.

Interpretation

  • Recovery appears metro-specific rather than a universal return to 2019.
  • Bus/ferry-oriented demand recovered better than commuter-heavy rail in SF Bay and Chicago.
  • Service and capital planning should use corridor/mode-specific recovery assumptions.
