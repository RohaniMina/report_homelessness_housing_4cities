Executive Summary: Homelessness and Housing (4 Cities)

  • PIT homelessness rose in all cities (2011->2024), largest in Chicago and NYC.
  • NYC/Chicago recent increases are mainly sheltered-system growth; LA rose in both sheltered and unsheltered counts.
  • Rent pressure rose in all metros (2015->2026).
  • 2026 annual comparable PIT not yet available in this run.

Data and Method

  • HUD PIT CoC annual counts, Zillow ZORI annualized, NYC daily shelter census.
  • Trend analysis, mayor segmentation, and 2022->2024 decomposition.

Core Findings

  • Chicago: 183.9% PIT change, unsheltered-share -17.3 pp.
  • New York City: 174.1% PIT change, unsheltered-share -2.0 pp.
  • Los Angeles: 105.7% PIT change, unsheltered-share 18.3 pp.
  • San Francisco: 46.8% PIT change, unsheltered-share -7.2 pp.
